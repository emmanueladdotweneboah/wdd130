Our History

Founded in 2010, Wasatch Accusystems has guided thrill-seekers through some of the most exciting whitewater adventures in Ghana. From humble beginnings with a single raft and a big dream, we've grown into a trusted outfitter with a passion for safety, fun, and unforgettable memories. Our guides are seasoned professionals dedicated to making your trip extraordinary.
